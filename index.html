About Dr. Sudheendra Huddar

Dr. Sudheendra Huddar is a highly skilled Consultant Psychiatrist with specialized expertise in Addiction Psychiatry. After completing his MBBS at BIMS, Belgaum, he pursued his post-graduate training in Psychiatry at the prestigious National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences (NIMHANS), Bengaluru.

He further honed his skills by obtaining a DM in Addiction Psychiatry from NIMHANS, making him one of the few specialists in this field. His comprehensive clinical experience spans over 7 years, during which he has worked at leading institutions like NIMHANS, AIIMS Delhi, and SDM Medical College, Dharwad.

Dr. Sudheendra is committed to providing a holistic and empathetic approach to mental health care. He believes in an open-minded evaluation of symptoms, maintaining a non-judgmental and compassionate attitude toward his clients.

Clinical Expertise & Areas of Specialization

Addiction Psychiatry

Expertise in managing all types of substance use disorders, including acute withdrawal management and long-term treatment.

Dual Diagnosis

Specializing in the treatment of co-occurring psychiatric disorders, such as depression with substance dependence.

Behavioral Addictions

Treatment for gambling addiction, excessive gaming, and other behavioral compulsions.

Adult ADHD

Diagnosis and treatment of Adult Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder.

Mood & Psychotic Disorders

Management of depressive disorders, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, and sexual dysfunction.

Sleep Disorders

Assessment and treatment of various sleep-related issues.
